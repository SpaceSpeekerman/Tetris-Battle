# Tetris Battle (C#)

A modern, fast, and precise take on classic Tetris.  
Built in C# with OpenTK, focused on tight controls, customization, and local multiplayer.

---

## Gameplay

- **Classic rules, modern features**
    
    - Ghost Piece (landing preview)
        
    - Hold Piece
        
    - Hard Drop
        
- **Responsive controls**
    
    - Keyboard + Gamepad support
        
    - DAS-style movement for fast play
        
- **Progressive difficulty**
    
    - Gravity increases with level
        
    - Board colors change over time (NES-inspired)
        

---

## Multiplayer

Local 1v1 play on the same screen.

- **Garbage system**: clear multiple lines to send penalty lines
    
- **Win modes**:
    
    - _Survival_: last player alive wins
        
    - _First Player Wins_: first top-out ends the round
        
- **Independent controls**: each player has their own bindings
    

---

## Options & Customization

- Toggle gameplay features:
    
    - Ghost Piece
        
    - Hold Piece
        
    - Hard Drop
        
    - Line Penalties
        
    - Infinite Level (fixed speed)
        
- **Full key rebinding**
    
- **Gamepad support**
    
    - D-Pad, sticks, triggers, shoulders
        

---

## Default Controls

|Action|Keyboard|Gamepad|
|---|---|---|
|Move|← →|D-Pad / Left Stick|
|Soft Drop|↓|D-Pad Down|
|Hard Drop|Space|D-Pad Up|
|Rotate|↑ / Z|A / B|
|Hold|C|Triggers / Shoulders|
|Pause|Esc|Start|
